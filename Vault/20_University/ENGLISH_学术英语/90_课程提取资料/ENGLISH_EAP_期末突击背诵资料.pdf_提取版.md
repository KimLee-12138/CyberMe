## 第3页

写作步骤  
1. 先给三篇资料贴标签： Source 1 = 优点 / 原因； Source 2 = 缺点 / 问题； Source 3 = 方法 / 解决。   
2. 第一句写总观点： The three sources suggest that...  
3. 中间整合三篇资料： Smith says A, while Lee says B, Wang further shows C.  
4. 最后一句下综合结论： Taken together / Therefore...
万能模板（约 120 词）  
Overall, the three sources suggest that [topic]  should be understood as [overall insight]  rather  
than a single issue. Smith (2024)  emphasizes that [Source 1 main idea] , showing the importance  
of [key point] . However / Similarly, Lee (2023)  points out that [Source 2 main idea] , which  
indicates [relationship: problem / support / contrast] . Wang (2022)  further extends this view by  
arguing that [Source 3 main idea] . Taken together, these sources indicate that [综合结论 ]. 
Therefore, a more balanced / comprehensive approach is needed to [solution or implication] .
常用  reporting verbs ： suggests, argues, reports, shows, finds, highlights, points out,  
demonstrates, warns.
4. Part V Abstract Writing ： 180–200 词结构式摘要  
必背框架  
1. Background ： 研究背景和问题   
2. Purpose ： 本研究想解决什么   
3. Methods ： 研究对象、数据来源、时间地点、分析方法   
4. Results： 主要发现，保留关键数字   
5. Conclusion ： 意义、应用、局限 / 未来研究
万能模板  
[Background]  X has become an important issue because it causes [problem] . [Purpose]  This  
study aimed to examine / evaluate / quantify whether [method/intervention]  could [effect] . 
[Methods]  Data were collected from [subjects/materials/sites]  during [time] . The researchers  
measured [variables]  and analyzed the data using [method/tool] . [Results]  The results showed  
that [major finding + data] . In addition, [second finding] . [Conclusion]  These findings suggest  
that [meaning/application] . However, because the study was limited by [limitation] , future  
research should [future direction] .
摘要高分提醒  
不要逐句抄原文，要  paraphrase 。   
Results 里一定写具体发现，给数字就保留数字。   
Discussion/Conclusion 要写意义： These findings suggest/support/indicate...  
不写个人评价： I think / in my opinion 全删。   
控制  180–200 words ，推荐  185 左右。

## 第4页

5. Part I 判断题  + 学术写作高频点  
正式学术风格：   
避免： I think, we/you 过多 , OK, lots of, rhetorical questions, contractions, proverbs 。   
使用： This study / The results / The findings / numerous / significant / indicate /  
demonstrate 。   
被动语态常用来强调  action/result ，不强调  doer 。   
Nominalization 名词化：  turn verbs/adjectives/clauses into nouns/nominal phrases ，使表达
更正式、更紧凑。例： analyze →  analysis ； decide →  decision ； because... →  due to...
Impersonal subject 非人称主语：  It is found that... / The results show... / This study  
examines... ，比  We found / You can see 更正式。
Cohesion & coherence ： 句子之间要有连接；同一段主题要一致；用  however, therefore,  
moreover, in contrast, taken together 。
